7-Day Peru Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Lima

🌅 Morning

Arrive in Lima at 6:30 AM, and take a morning flight to Cusco (1-hour flight).

🌞 Afternoon

After arriving in Cusco, take a 1.5-hour car/bus ride to Ollantaytambo.

🌙 Evening

Explore the Ollantaytambo ruins, and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere of this small town.

Day 2: Machu Picchu

🌅 Morning

Take an early train from Ollantaytambo to Aguas Calientes (1.5 hours), then catch the bus to Machu Picchu.

🌞 Afternoon

Spend the day exploring Machu Picchu, with options for hiking Huayna Picchu or Machu Picchu Mountain.

🌙 Evening

Return to Ollantaytambo by train in the evening.

Day 3: Sacred Valley to Cusco

🌅 Morning

Travel from Ollantaytambo to Cusco (1.5 hours by car or bus).

🌞 Afternoon

Visit Plaza de Armas, Qorikancha, and Sacsayhuamán ruins in Cusco.

🌙 Evening

Dinner and relaxation in the San Blas district.

Day 4: 7 Lakes of Ausangate Hike

🌅 Morning

Depart early for a 7 Lakes of Ausangate hike (about 3-4 hours by car).

🌞 Afternoon

Hike and explore the stunning lakes and Ausangate Mountain.

🌙 Evening

Return to Cusco for a well-deserved rest.

Day 5: Rainbow Mountain

🌅 Morning

Early departure for the Rainbow Mountain trek (3-hour drive).

🌞 Afternoon

Trek to the top of Vinicunca to see Rainbow Mountain and enjoy the stunning scenery.

🌙 Evening

Return to Cusco in the late afternoon.

Day 6: Cusco to Lima

🌅 Morning

Take a morning flight from Cusco to Lima (1-hour flight).

🌞 Afternoon

Explore Miraflores, Plaza Mayor, and San Francisco Monastery.

🌙 Evening

Dinner in Barranco, enjoying ocean views.

Day 7: Huacachina Day Trip

🌅 Morning

Take a morning bus from Lima to Huacachina (4-5 hours).

🌞 Afternoon

Enjoy a dune buggy ride and sandboarding in Huacachina, explore Ica, or visit a local vineyard.

🌙 Evening

Return to Lima in the evening (4-5 hours by bus).
